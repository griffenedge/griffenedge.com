Mapping the community's needs and responses to COVID-19.

[Live prototype](https://pledgeprotect.netlify.app/)

## Type

Project for [DECO3200: Interactive Product Design Studio](https://www.sydney.edu.au/units/DECO3200) at the University of Sydney

## Duration

August to November 2020

## Role

<strong>Designer</strong> on a team of 3 (myself, Nancy Nguyen, and [Raunak Sitoula](https://www.raunaksitoula.com/)). My responsibilities included user research, ideation, persona generation, user testing, video production, prototyping and interface design.

## Summary

Pledge &amp; Protect is a community mapping of loved ones that need to be protected from COVID-related complications and people who pledge to practice physical distancing and other COVID-safe behaviours.
